Welcome to this help for the use of the page: Spoonerism aid

First of all, choose the type of spoonerism you want by clicking
on the "Letters" button or the "Phonemes" button

Comes the turn of the control panel. You can
modify 4 fields: the number of letters to subtract from the entered word,
from the searched word (quite self-explanatory). The min/max length of the 2 pairs corresponds to
the desired length range during the final display of the 4 words.
You can also check/uncheck the corresponding box to
enable/disable the search in the cut words.

The Statistics panel allows you to observe
execution time according to the parameters you have entered in the configuration panel.
(it would be better to change the parameters if the execution takes several minutes!)

Then fill in a word, then start the search.
A list of results is then displayed: click on your favourite!

Here is an example of a display, after clicking on the "hair - pair" button.
You can see the 2 words of the initial sentence on the line above,
and the 2 words of the final sentence after exchange of letters below.

If you know the 2 words to search for, just write
the word to search for in the "Optional" field.
The final display will be as if you had clicked on the button containing
the 2 words (here "hair", and "pair" optional).

I hope this page has helped you! Good looking for spoonerisms ;)
